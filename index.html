About Me

On May 3, 2021, I graduated from the University of Pittsburgh with a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering and a minor in Computer Science! As a very recent grad, I'm both excited and nervous to see where life will take me next. The COVID-19 pandemic has changed the circumstances of living life and I can only hope to learn how to navigate our new reality.

I'm originally from Northern California, about an hour outside of San Francisco, however I've spent the past 3-4 years in Pittsburgh, PA studying Electrical Engineering at the Swanson School of Engineering at the University of Pittsburgh. Through summer research and internship experiences, I've been given the opportunity to travel to other parts of the U.S. and have gained an appreciation of travel that has been momentarily put on hold due to COVID-19.

I hope to go back to school in a couple of years to pursue a Master's degree in either Electrical Engineering or Computer Science after gaining some hands-on experience in a workplace setting. I'm aiming towards developing my programming skills and knowledge about information security and cybersecurity. My last semester of university coursework sparked a greater interest in the intersection of hardware and software and I look forward to learning more about this field through real-world applications such as autonomous vehicles, IoT, and network security.
